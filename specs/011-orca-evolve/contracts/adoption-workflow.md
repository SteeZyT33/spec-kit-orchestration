# Contract: Adoption Workflow

## Purpose

Define the operator workflow for capturing and evolving adoption decisions.

## Required Steps

1. capture a harvested idea as a durable entry
2. assign an explicit adoption decision
3. map the entry into an Orca destination or mark it deferred/rejected
4. update the entry when the mapped Orca work advances

## V1 Expectations

- workflow must remain explicit and inspectable
- deferred and rejected entries remain visible for future review
- implemented entries keep their source attribution and rationale
